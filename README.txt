To run the game:

i) access the command line

ii) type: make [ENTER]

iii) type: ./run [ENTER]

Enhancements:

- added health bar at top left of screen which depletes whenever the helicopter
  is hit by a missile, or whenever the helicopter hits the top of a building.

- added health orbs which replenish your health bar.

- you acquire points whenever you hit a missile silo. The points will float upwards until they
  leave the screen.

- added rockets to the arsenal of the helicopter.

- blocks break when hit with rockets, bombs, or rammed with the helicopter.
